    CAN Backpack

    $14

    Description

    Hook up your Teensy to a Controller Area Network (CAN) Bus with our CAN Backpack! CAN Bus is commonly found in automobiles, robots, and drones, but it is useful anytime a robust, long distance bus architecture is needed.

    We integrated the MCP2562 CAN Bus Transceiver along with six headers for easily implementing star topologies or a line topology without needing T-connectors. Each header contains power and ground along with the CAN signal lines for easily powering your CAN nodes.

    Several CAN Arduino libraries are available for Teensy, including FlexCAN and UAVCAN.

    The CAN Backpack comes fully assembled and tested.

    Check out all of our wonderfully small and powerful Teensy Backpacks!

    Teensy Backpacks

    Arduino has shields, Feather has wings, Raspberry Pi has hats, BeagleBone Black has capes, and now Teensy has backpacks! At Bolder Flight Systems, we love the Teensy and have used it extensively for everything from our Research and Development Flight Control Systems to motor and servo test stands, prototyping, and our personal hobby projects. It has the perfect combination of size, power, cost, and support.

    We’re extremely excited to be the first to offer stacking add-on boards for Teensy! Matching the Teensy 3.2 footprint, these high quality boards extend the utility of your Teensy and are perfect for embedding Teensy in your projects. We’ve carefully designed each board to integrate well with each other, so you can mix and match boards without worry. See our Teensy Backpacks Pinout page for more information on how they stack together.

    These initial boards are perfect for robotics and drones. We’ll be continuing to develop additional backpacks, including: wifi, BTLE, LoRa, 900 MHz and 433 MHz radios, and lipo charging. Please email us at info@bolderflight.com with additional backpack suggestions.
